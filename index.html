WHY?

It has become very common, individuals who wish to buy or sell crypto are scammed on a daily basis by people who claim to have funds but in the end are simple criminals, but we aim to stop this.
With state of the act blockchain technology you can verify if a wallet is owned by a user and avoid any problem that may arise due to dishonesty on one side of the trading party.

HOW TO USE

For Mobile Users

Open your wallet application (the one containing the funds you wish to sell) and go to the browser.
In the browser input the link and visit the DAPP.
Now you can click verify your assets. You can then send a screenshot of the page to your trading patner

For Desktop Users

Visit the website on your browser (the one that has your wallet attached to it).
Connect your wallet when you receive the prompt.
